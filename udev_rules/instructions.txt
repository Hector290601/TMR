You need to copy the 80-mha-Robot.rules to the /etc/udev/rules.d/ folder, you can do it with:

sudo cp 80-mhaRobot.rules /etc/udev/rules.d/

Next, you need to reolad your udevrules on the system, using:

sudo udevadm control --reload-rules && sudo service udev restart && sudo udevadm trigger

Then, the roboclaw and the hokuyo will be recognized with symlinks.
